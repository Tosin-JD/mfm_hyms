1504

IRISH C.M.


1.
To Thee, O comforter divine
for all thy grace and pow'r be nign
Sing we Alleluia!

2.
To Thee, whose faithful love had place
In God's great covenant of grace
Sing we Alleluia!

3.
To Thee, whose faithful pow'r
Doth heal enlighten, sanctify and seal
Sing we Alleluia!

4.
To the, our teacher and our friend
Our faithful leader to the end
Sing we Alleluia!

5.
To Thee, by Jesus Christ sent down
Of all his gifts the sun and crown
Sing we Alleluia!

- Frances R. Havergal
