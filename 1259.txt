1259
Haslemere - 5.5.5.5.

1.
God whose name is Love,
Happy children we:
Listen to the hymn
That we sing to thee.

2.
Help us to be good,
Always kind and true,
In the games we play
Or the work we do.

3.
Bless us every one
Singing here to thee.
God whose name is Love,
Loving may we be!

- Florence Hoatson
